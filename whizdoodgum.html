WhizDood Gum Rubber Duck Edition

Chew on the quackiest flavor in town with Whizdoods Gum: Rubber Duck Edition! This playful gum is packed with a whimsical burst of flavor inspired by everyone's favorite bath-time buddy—the rubber duck. With every chew, you'll be greeted by a bouncy, squeaky-smooth sensation that combines a light, rubbery essence with a splash of bubbly fun. It’s a quirky, quirky twist on gum that’s sure to float your taste buds into a world of childhood nostalgia. Perfect for those who love a little weird in their chew, Whizdoods Gum: Rubber Duck Edition is here to make your day a bit more buoyant!
